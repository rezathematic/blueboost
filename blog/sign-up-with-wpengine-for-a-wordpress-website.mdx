## Receiving Your WP Engine Confirmation Email

### Checking Your Inbox

After completing the payment, check your email inbox for a confirmation message
from WP Engine. This email will contain important information about your new
account.

### What to Look for in the Confirmation Email

The confirmation email typically includes your account details, login
information, and a link to access your WP Engine dashboard. Keep this email for
your records.

### Troubleshooting Missing Confirmation Emails

If you don’t receive the confirmation email within a few minutes, check your
spam or junk folder. If it’s still missing, contact WP Engine support for
assistance.
